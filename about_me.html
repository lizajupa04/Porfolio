ABOUT ME


Hi, I’m Liza from Colombia. I’m a civil engineer and specialist in Project Management with more that 3 years of experience in the industry. Due the pandemic, many people's lives changed, including mine.

So I started working teaching Spanish on a platform called Preply, where I was able to improve my skills to communicate in another language, then I decided to emigrate to the United States to work as an Au Pair, improve my English and get to know a new culture.

While I was living in USA, I started to create my new project, which was based on the creation of a website and as I didn't have much knowledge in programming I started to learn about this world through Youtube and at the same time looking up differents colleges to study something related with programming and finally I got a Workshop from Shecodes where I got certified as a Front-End Developer.

I’m currently studying at Coursera UI/UX Design, because I think it is a very good complement for a Front-End Developer and because one of my dream is to be able to work with companies from other countries
